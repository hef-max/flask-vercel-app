Welcome to the MindCura community. Our community is built on a foundation of safety, mutual support, and empathy. These guidelines are established to maintain healthy, respectful, and positive interactions across the MindCura platform.

1. Respect the Privacy and Boundaries of Others

Do not share other people's personal information without permission. All interactions must maintain ethics and confidentiality.

2. No Hate Speech or Discrimination

Any form of hate speech, racism, sexism, homophobia, or other discrimination will not be tolerated.

3. Be Kind and Supportive

Remember that every user comes with different circumstances and experiences. Show empathy and do not judge.

4. Do Not Provide Diagnoses or Therapy

Users are not allowed to provide mental health diagnoses or medical advice to others, unless you are a verified professional who has been officially requested to do so.

5. Avoid Spam and Irrelevant Promotions

Do not send links, promotions, or invitations that are not related to mental health topics and community activities.

6. Report Disturbing Content

If you find content that violates these guidelines, use the reporting feature or contact our team directly.

7. A Safe Space for All

MindCura is committed to creating a safe space for all productive ages. There are no stupid questions, and every story deserves to be valued.

8. Consequences of Violations

Any violation of these guidelines may result in warnings, feature restrictions, or permanent account deletion.

By using the MindCura app and community features, you agree to comply with all these guidelines to create a healthy and supportive environment together.
