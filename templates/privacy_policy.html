We respect your privacy. This document explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when using the MindCura app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, age, gender, and account information when you register.
  • Interaction Data: Conversation notes with MIRA, journaling results, meditation audios used, and daily habit patterns.
  • Emotion Data: Analysis of facial expressions, voice, and daily mood input if the user has activated the feature.
  • Screening Data: Psychological test results such as the DASS-21 and reflection scores from our system.
  • Technical Data: Device information such as device type, OS, and city location (without detailed GPS).

2. How We Use Information

  • Providing a personalized in-app experience
  • Generating psychological reports (Mind Report, Mind Progress)
  • Providing personalized reflection and exercise suggestions
  • Conducting internal analysis for AI system development
  • Maintaining account security and preventing misuse

3. Data Protection

Your data is stored encrypted and is not shared with third parties without your permission. We implement high security standards to protect your information from unauthorized access.

4. Your Data Rights

  • You can access and review your data through your profile page
  • You can request deletion of your account and all data at any time through the settings menu or by contacting us

5. Use by Minors

This application is only intended for users aged 18 years and over, or at least 17 years old with the consent of a guardian.

6. Third Parties

We do not sell, share, or exchange your personal data with third parties. However, we may use third-party services (such as Google Analytics or Firebase) to assist with technical operations, while maintaining data confidentiality.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy
